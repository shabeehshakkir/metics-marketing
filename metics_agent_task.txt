# FAQs

**Can suppliers bid partially?**
Yes. Buyers can enable partial bidding; suppliers then quote partial quantities. Buyers can also make partial awards across suppliers.

**Can I restrict an RFQ to selected suppliers?**
Yes. Choose Closed (selected/authorized suppliers) or Open (all in category).

**Do POs generate automatically?**
Yes. Awarding a bid auto‚Äëgenerates a purchase order with agreed terms; buyers can export a PDF.

**Can I edit an RFQ after publishing?**
Before the deadline: yes (with notifications to participants). Critical fields may lock after bids arrive to keep fairness.

**Can I integrate with my ERP?**
Yes, import RFQs via CSV or API; mapping templates supported.

**How do suppliers get RFQs?**
By category matching after they set company & product categories.

**Is there in‚Äëplatform communication?**
Yes, real‚Äëtime chat on each RFQ, plus notifications and an updates feed.
